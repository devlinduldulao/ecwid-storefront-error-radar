Getting Started

Three steps to your first storefront diagnostic — no server or database required.

1
Open the Dashboard

You are here. This page is your diagnostic command center inside the Ecwid admin panel.

2
Browse Your Store Preview

Scroll to Embedded Store Preview below and navigate through products, cart, and checkout. Errors and slow requests are captured automatically as you browse.

3
Review & Save Results

Check the Session Summary for the overview and the Incident Feed for details. Click Save to History to keep a record in this browser for later reference.

Tip: Use Demo Preview to explore the dashboard with simulated data before running a real scan.

Privacy: This app runs entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device and no live shoppers are monitored.
